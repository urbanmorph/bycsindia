BYCS INDIA FOUNDATION

GLOBAL INITIATIVE

BYCS India Foundation is a nonpartisan organisation that aims to improve the quality of life for Indian citizens through national and city level initiatives around cycling as urban transportation.

We support cities to advance a cycling friendly future through planning, education, advocacy, and innovative program development. We coordinate the Bicycle Mayor & Leaders Network at the national level, representing more than 40 cities across 16 states.

FOCUS AREAS

PEOPLE

We engage with civil society to enact behavioural change and make cycling for daily trips safe and comfortable for all.

GOVERNMENT

We lobby for better bicycling infrastructure and policies in the country, and support programs and planning that place people at the heart of our streets and cities.

INNOVATION

We leverage technology to make data-driven decisions and scale impact driven initiatives to facilitate cycling.
