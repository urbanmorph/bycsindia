Cities Fit For Kids

We are working to include bicycling into the cirriculum so we can get the children make the right choices when they grow up. There is a global movement towards safer streets for kids. Are you an educator looking to get interesting content to kids? talk to us to understand how we can help.

Neighbourhood Cycling

All change starts with the tiniest of steps. Break free from the shackles of inaction and get on the bicycle around your neighbourhood. Cycle Days and open streets events have taken the concept of reclaiming streets to the neighbourhood in many cities across the country. There is extensive experience within BYCS India to deliver open streets and measure impact. Talk to us if you wish to have open streets in your city.

Cycle To Work

The CycleToWork campaign has provided the much needed peak hour offset in many of our cities. It has decreased pollution, increased productivity and created a momentum that has made cities across the country think about safe cycling infrastructure. We now have a global working group to focus on creating impact. Talk to us to know more.

Emergency Response

From delivering essential supplies like medicine and groceries to books to food, the bicycle has helped bring relief to many people during this pandemic.
